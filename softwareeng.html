Previous Career in Software Engineering

Prior to my graduate study at Duke University, I was a software engineer. As an undergraduate at Michigan State University, I spent the bulk of my time preparing myself for a software engineering career. My coursework, out-of-course studying of the latest industry trends, and active involvement in student professional organizations, culminated in my above-average career placement for a graduate of Michigan State University's College of Engineering. I left college with the expectation that I would be climbing the corporate software engineering ladder for the rest of my life. After much delibration, I realized my primary interests lie elsewhere.

I'm grateful for having gained expertise in technical communication, the ability to manage large scale projects, and top notch programming skills. I've also been part of and seen corporate innovation, strategy, and automation, first hand. Below, I list some of my experiences.
