Research

Below is some notable ongoing projects -- omitting previous class projects etc.
  • Duke AME Lab
    I am the primary owner and maintainer of the DAME-FLAME Python Package. The paper based on this package is here. Advised by Drs. Cynthia Rudin, Alex Volfovksy, Sudeepa Roy. Additionally exploring several additional research projects with the lab, both applied and theoretical.
  • Ethics and ML Project
    Advised by Dr. Hari Subramonyam at Stanford University, working actively on a paper undergoing submission.
  • United Nations Global Pulse Lab
    I worked on modelling covid spread in Cox's Bazar, Bangladesh, and contributed to research design on an experiment tackling vaccine hesitancy.
  • Self-Directed Ongoing Works
    My ongoing independent work include (1) special ability immigration policies primarily in the tech sector and impacts on wage gaps, (2) risk assessment scoring systems and fairness.
  • Duke University ML in Agricultural Economics Project
    I'm coathoring a team paper undergoing submission, on trends in Agricultural Economics Research over the last century, after managing a team of summer undergraduate interns in NLP and Economics research. With Drs. Norbert Wilson, Leslie Collins, Boyla Mainsah, et al.
